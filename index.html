8th International Conference on Energy, Environment & Sustainable Development — EESD 2026

Machine Learning-Based Rainfall Prediction for the Sukkur Region Using Long-Term Climatic Data

Developed a temporal machine learning pipeline using 51 years of historical climate data (1970?2020) to forecast monthly rainfall in semi-arid zones. Proved that Support Vector Regression with an RBF kernel significantly outperforms traditional statistical models, and used SHAP analysis to identify maximum temperature as the strongest driver of monsoon variance.

The framework is designed to support the Sindh PDMA in flood mitigation planning and local agricultural authorities in irrigation scheduling.
